Stephanie Chetrit

General and Colorectal Surgeon

MBBS FRACS

Dr Stephanie Chetrit is an experienced general surgeon specialising in colorectal surgery, laparoscopic management of colorectal cancer, irritable bowel disease and pelvic floor conditions such as incontinence and obstructive defecation. She also performs general and acute care surgery.

Dr Chetrit completed her training locally and commenced working as a general surgeon at Royal Perth Hospital (RPH). She then relocated overseas for two years of further training in colorectal surgery.
